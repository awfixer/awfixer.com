## Key Features

### Advanced Privacy Controls

AWBrowse gives you complete control over your privacy:

- **Tracker Blocking**: Automatically blocks tracking scripts and pixels
- **Cookie Management**: Intelligent cookie handling with automatic cleanup
- **Referrer Protection**: Prevents websites from seeing where you came from
- **WebRTC Leak Protection**: Stops your real IP from being exposed
- **Canvas Fingerprinting Protection**: Blocks attempts to uniquely identify your browser

### Built-in Tools

No need for extensions - everything you need is included:

- **Password Manager**: Secure, encrypted password storage
- **VPN Integration**: Built-in VPN for enhanced privacy (premium feature)
- **Download Manager**: Advanced download control and organization
- **Screenshot Tool**: Capture full pages or selected areas
- **Developer Tools**: Complete suite for web developers

### Smart Features

- **Intelligent Search**: Privacy-focused search with multiple engine options
- **Tab Management**: Advanced tab grouping and organization
- **Reading Mode**: Distraction-free article reading
- **Night Mode**: Easy on the eyes for low-light browsing
- **Sync Across Devices**: Encrypted synchronization of bookmarks and settings

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is AWBrowse really free?

Yes! AWBrowse is completely free and always will be. We believe privacy is a fundamental right, not a premium feature. Some advanced features like premium VPN servers may require a subscription, but core privacy protection is always free.

### How does AWBrowse make money?

We operate on a sustainable model:
- Optional premium features (advanced VPN, cloud sync storage)
- Privacy-respecting search partnerships
- Corporate licensing for organizations
- Community donations and support

We never sell your data or compromise your privacy for profit.

### Can I import my data from other browsers?

Absolutely! AWBrowse makes it easy to migrate:
- Bookmarks and passwords
- Browsing history (stored locally only)
- Extensions and settings
- Search engines and preferences

### Is AWBrowse compatible with Chrome extensions?

Yes! AWBrowse supports most Chrome extensions while adding additional privacy protections. Some extensions that collect data may be blocked or modified to protect your privacy.
