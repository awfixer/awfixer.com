## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Modded Android safe to use?
Yes, when installed properly. We provide verified builds with security patches and regular updates. However, like any custom ROM, it requires technical knowledge to install safely.

### Will I lose my warranty?
Yes, installing custom ROMs typically voids manufacturer warranties. Some regions have "right to repair" laws that may protect you, but this varies by location.

### Can I still use Google services?
You can optionally install MicroG (an open-source Google services replacement) or use Aurora Store to access Play Store apps without a Google account.

### How often are updates released?
We aim for monthly security updates and quarterly feature updates. Critical security patches are released as needed.

### Can I go back to stock Android?
Yes, you can usually restore stock firmware using manufacturer tools. However, some devices may retain evidence of modification.
