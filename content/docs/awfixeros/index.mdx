# Introduction to AWFixerOS

<Callout type="info">
  **Note:** These guides are being written during development. Some features and instructions may change as we continue to improve AWFixerOS.
</Callout>

AWFixerOS is our custom Linux-based operating system designed with privacy, security, and user experience at its core. Built on solid foundations, AWFixerOS provides a secure computing environment while maintaining the flexibility and power that Linux users expect.

## What is AWFixerOS?

AWFixerOS is a carefully crafted Linux distribution that combines:

- **Privacy-first approach** - Your data stays yours
- **Enhanced security** - Built-in protections against common threats
- **User-friendly interface** - Easy for beginners, powerful for experts
- **Open source transparency** - Community-driven development
- **Hardware compatibility** - Works on a wide range of devices

## Philosophy

At AWFixer and Friends, we believe that your operating system should:

- **Respect your privacy** - No telemetry, no data mining
- **Protect your security** - Proactive security measures
- **Empower your creativity** - Tools for makers, creators, and developers
- **Stay out of your way** - Simple, efficient, and reliable
