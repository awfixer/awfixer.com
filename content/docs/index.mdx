## What is AWFixer and Friends?

AWFixer and Friends is a company founded by AWFixer, jinx and the friends with a founding goal of making the world a better place. We do not have a goal of making money, and the people who use our products will always matter more to us than our bottom line. We are a company that makes things that matter for people who matter and is run by friends who care.

## Found an Issue?

Don't be afraid to submit a change request for this help center [on our GitHub repository](https://github.com/awfixer-org/awfixer.com).

Need immediate help? Join our [Discord community](https://discord.awfixer.com/) where our team and community members are ready to assist you.
